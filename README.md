# Financial Health Analysis of Public Companies

## Objective
Evaluate the financial health of selected publicly listed companies using
key financial ratios derived from income statements, balance sheets,
and cash flow statements.

# Scope
JPM – JPMorgan Chase
BAC – Bank of America
C – Citigroup
WFC – Wells Fargo
GS – Goldman Sachs


## Objective
Evaluate the financial health of selected publicly listed companies using
key financial ratios derived from income statements, balance sheets,
and cash flow statements.

## Dataset
- Source: SEC EDGAR / Yahoo Finance
- Companies analyzed:
  - JPM – JPMorgan Chase
  - BAC – Bank of America
  - C – Citigroup
  - WFC – Wells Fargo
  - GS – Goldman Sachs
- Time period: 2021–2024

## Methodology
1. Data collection and validation
2. Data cleaning and standardization
3. KPI computation
4. Visualization and interpretation

## Key KPIs
- Liquidity: Current Ratio
- Profitability: Net Profit Margin, ROA, ROE
- Solvency: Debt-to-Equity
